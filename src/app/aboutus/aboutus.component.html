Who we are?

Crescent Technologies (CT) is an independent healthcare technology and innovation consultancy firm. We provide total and creative solutions to our clients, mainly in the healthcare and medical industry. Our consultations cover a broad spectrum of activities ranging from asset management to enterprise resource planning. Our experts will assist you in a number of strategic and operational management issues ranging from the evaluation of repair and maintenance jobs performed in your hospital, to restructuring your organizational processes and adding value to your supply chain system, to planning future expansions of your business. Whatever your Management and technology needs are, we are there to give you full support.

Mission & vision

• Mission

Supplying operational and strategic management consultancy, providing technology and innovation solutions and studies, and delivering training services that meet the needs of the healthcare industry throughout its whole supply chain. This is to be achieved by a customer-centric attitude, coupled with the use and implementation of state of the art technologies, while making an efficient use of our front and back-office resources.

• Vision

To become one of the top-ranking consulting firms in the healthcare field in the Middle East & North Africa (MENA) region.
